With Hawaii united under Kamehameha, time devoted to infighting was allocated to further developing their culture. Due to the relatively peaceful times gaining Mana from military victory and cannibalism was a rare occurrence. Instead the Hawaiians transitioned from violence to harmony, developing a new philosophy and a new way to gain Mana known as "The Aloha Spirit."

Aloha in its most basic definition means both hello and goodbye, however, it is also a way of life. To follow the way of Aloha, is to find harmony with the natural world. This includes people, animals, and the environment. With the advent of Aloha, native Hawaiians focused more on respecting nature and trying to preserve its pristine condition. They adopted a manner of hospitality that Hawaiians are now famed for. It is partly because of Aloha that the Hawaiian Islands today are one of the best vacation destinations in the world.
